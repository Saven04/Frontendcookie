Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 08, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NewsHub ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, [yourwebsite.com], and use our services, including cookie management and location data features. By using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email address provided during multi-factor authentication (MFA) or account actions.
  • Cookie Preferences: Your consent ID and preferences (e.g., strictly necessary, performance, functional, advertising, social media).
  • Location Data: IP address, ISP, city, country, latitude, and longitude, linked to your consent ID.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with our site (e.g., pages visited), collected via cookies if consented.
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our news delivery and user settings.
  • Authenticate deletion requests via MFA.
  • Manage cookie preferences.
  • Analyze usage (if performance cookies are enabled).
  • Ensure security and legal compliance.

4. Cookie Management

4.1 Types of Cookies

We use these cookie categories, manageable in our settings:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for functionality (e.g., authentication). Always enabled.
  • Performance: Tracks analytics (optional).
  • Functional: Enhances features (optional).
  • Advertising: Targets ads (optional).
  • Social Media: Integrates social platforms (optional).

4.2 Managing Preferences

You can update preferences in the "Settings" section. Non-essential cookies are off by default unless opted-in. Deleting cookie data resets non-essential preferences to "off," preserving your consent ID and strictly necessary settings.

4.3 Retention

Cookie preferences are kept for 2 years unless deleted. Upon deletion request, non-essential preferences are disabled immediately, with records removed after 2 years.

5. Location Data Management

5.1 Collection and Use

Location data (IP address, ISP, city, country, latitude, longitude) is collected for service enhancement and security, tied to your consent ID.

5.2 Deletion

Upon a "Delete Cookie Data" request, location data is marked as deleted with a timestamp. It is retained for 90 days for security purposes, during which it is not processed, and is permanently deleted thereafter.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your data. It may be shared with:

  • Service Providers: For hosting, email (MFA), and analytics (if consented).
  • Legal Obligations: As required by law or for safety.
  • Business Transfers: In mergers or acquisitions.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have these rights:

  • Access: Request your data (e.g., cookie preferences, location).
  • Rectification: Correct inaccuracies.
  • Erasure: Request deletion—non-essential cookies reset immediately, location data retained 90 days.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit data use.
  • Object: Oppose processing (e.g., analytics).
  • Data Portability: Get your data in a readable format.

Contact us at privacy@newshub.com to exercise these rights.

8. Data Security

We use encryption and access controls to protect your data, though no online system is fully secure.

9. Third-Party Links

Links to third-party sites (e.g., news sources) are not covered by this policy.
